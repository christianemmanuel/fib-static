We understand the drive needed to grow successful enterprises. Our own success as the first internet bank (yes, we were the trailblazers!) was, and still is, fueled by that same drive. So we’ve developed a full range of commercial banking services to help businesses better manage their resources. 

Whether you want to earn more on your excess cash, need to make better use of your working capital, or seek assistance with your payment systems, First Internet Bank is will consult with you to provide convenient, flexible and practical solutions that are tailored to your unique business needs. 

Our Commercial Banking team works primarily with privately held businesses with annual revenues ranging from $2 million to $50 million, within a three-hour radius of Indianapolis. We serve clients from a broad range of industries including (but not limited to): ​​​​​​​

  • Manufacturers
  • Professional services
  • Nonprofit organizations
  • Property management
  • Distributors
  • Technology

Deposits

For a growing enterprise, time and money are two resources that are always precious. And making the most of both is critical. At First Internet Bank, our deposit products for commercial enterprises make everyday banking easy and convenient. And with our money market, CD, and checking accounts, you can access our attractive interest rates and a wide range of features to help build and leverage your capital. Learn More
