Debit Card

Avoid the hassle of writing checks or carrying cash for everyday purchases. The First Internet Bank debit card is accepted everywhere VISA is accepted.

Add your First Internet Bank Visa card to Apple Pay through the Wallet app to make your purchases with a single touch anywhere that Apple Pay is accepted. Just place your finger on the Touch ID and hold your phone near the card reader. A receipt will even be captured in the Wallet app, to help you keep track of your purchases.

Withdraw cash using your debit card at ATMs displaying the PLUS symbol, more than a million worldwide. Best of all, we won’t charge you for it — and we’ll even reimburse you up to $10 per month for ATM fees from the owner of the ATM. We also offer ATM-only cards that allow you to withdraw cash from ATMs, but cannot be used for purchases.
