Everything you need and nothing you don’t

At First Internet Bank, we offer a full range of banking solutions, from checking accounts and high-yield savings to CDs and mortgages. We don’t have an expensive branch network to maintain, so we pass our savings on to you in the form of better rates, lower fees and a full suite of free online and mobile banking tools, including an intuitive personal financial management solution.

Access your accounts from the palm of your hand (anytime, anywhere)

Download the free First Internet Bank mobile app to see basic account information, check your balance, deposit a check, view recent transactions and transfer funds with just a few taps. Or get a snapshot of your account balance and activity with text message banking. 

  • A branch in your pocket

Savings Accounts

Not just for a rainy day! Our savings accounts pay competitive interest rates and are tightly integrated with our other accounts for quick real-time fund transfers. And as always, our savings accounts are FDIC-insured. With our great rates, you can watch your funds grow any day, no matter what the weather looks like. Learn More

Checking & Debit

We’re guessing that convenient, easy-to-use checking and everyday banking sounds good to you. That’s why we offer free debit cards and multiple options for making deposits to help you securely manage and move your money. From making deposits to paying bills to shopping online, we’ve got you covered when it comes to convenience and security. To safeguard your finances and avoid overdraft fees, take advantage of optional overdraft protection by linking your First Internet Bank Checking Account to your First Internet Bank Savings or Money Market account. CDs

Looking for a virtually risk-free savings option? Open a high-yield, FDIC-insured Certificate of Deposit (CD) with as little as $1,000 at First Internet Bank and take advantage of a predictable return, with no maintenance fees and fixed rates that are typically higher than traditional Savings and Money Market accounts. Health Savings Accounts

Take the worry out of paying for healthcare expenses and save for your qualified medical costs with a First Internet Bank Health Savings Account (HSA). Use this tax-advantaged account to cover eligible medical expenses. Plus, there’s no maintenance fee, funds can roll over from one year to the next and your contributions are portable — the money is yours even if you change employment. Learn More

IRAs
